What is Current?

The room you've been looking for.

Current at UBC is a student-run keynote and networking event that gives UBC students rare access to the people shaping Vancouver's finance and venture capital landscape. This isn't a lecture. It's a day where ideas, ambition, and generosity share the same room.

★

Keynote

Three keynote sessions from Vancouver's top founders, VCs, and finance leaders. Real insight, no fluff.

▶

Connect

A live networking floor with 50 professionals from Vancouver's finance, VC, and tech industries. Real conversations. Real connections.

♥

Charity

Every ticket sold generates a direct donation. All net proceeds go to BC Children's Hospital Foundation.
